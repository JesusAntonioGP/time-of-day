This is a JavaScript page which uses the JS Date Object Model is meant to create a dynamic page depening on the time of day that the webpage is accessed.

The requirements of this page are as follows: display a unique image, color, and message for the following times of day:

  • Morning 5 am - 12 pm
  • Afternoon 12 pm - 6 pm
  • Evening 6 pm - 11 pm
  • Night 11 pm - 5 am
